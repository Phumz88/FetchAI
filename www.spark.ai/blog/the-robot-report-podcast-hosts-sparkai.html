The Robot Report Hosts SparkAI

Fetch.AI CEO Michael Kohen joins The Robot Report Podcast to discuss the impact of the edge case problem on AI robotics.

Fetch.AI CEO Michael Kohen sat down with hosts of The Robot Report Podcast, Steve Crowe and Mike Oitzman, to discuss the edge case problem in robotics and AI.

Michael outlined the unexpected situations in which AI and machine learning robotics systems falter or fail, and how Fetch.AI overcomes these unexpected situations by resolving edge cases in real-time. 

He shared specific real-world robotic examples, including details about SparkAI’s partnership with John Deere and its autonomous tractors.
